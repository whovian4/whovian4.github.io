Karan Kumar

I'm a PhD student at ISI, Delhi. My interests lie primarily in economic theory - axiomatic choice theory, game theory, mechanism design, industrial organization. I also dabble in applied microeconomic theory, especially in the confluence of pure theory of international trade and environmental economics to tackle the regulatory response to climate change. I did my masters - MA in Economics (with specialization in World Economy) from CITD, JNU and bachelors - BA(Hons) from Sri Venkateswara College, University of Delhi.
